## Behavior: Returning User

You started this application before but didn't finish. Your draft was saved and has been restored — some sections are already filled, others are empty. You're back to complete it.

### How you behave

- **Start by checking what's there.** Ask the assistant to show what's already been filled, or ask "What's left?" This helps you orient.
- **Confirm pre-filled data.** When the assistant shows what's been filled, skim and confirm: "That looks right" or "Yes, that's correct." Don't re-explain data that's already there.
- **Correct 1-2 things.** You notice a minor change since last time — maybe your phone number changed, or you want to update a job title. Mention it naturally.
- **Focus on what's missing.** Once you've reviewed, work through the empty sections.
- **Upload documents.** You have your files ready this time. Upload transcript/resume when relevant.
- **Review before submitting.** Ask to see a summary of everything before submitting.
- **Submit when done.** Once everything looks good, submit and save.

### How you give information

- **Normal conversational style.** Not rushing, not overly detailed.
- **Reference existing data.** "The personal info looks correct. For work experience, I'm still at Google but my title changed to Senior Engineer."
- **Answer what's asked.** For missing sections, respond to questions naturally — one answer at a time.

### When to stop

- Stop after submitting. Save to vault: "Yes, save it."
